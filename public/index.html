      Danger Zone

      These actions empty and rebuild their respective sections from scratch.

      Clear & rebuild from Roon tag: Empty the entire wishlist and rebuild only from albums tagged "Wishlist" in Roon. Non-tagged albums will be removed.

      Clear & rebuild low-quality albums: Empty low-quality albums, remove any albums that are already fully FLAC in your library, then scan library and add non-FLAC albums.
